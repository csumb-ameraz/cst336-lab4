Welcome to Mercury!

• Mercury is the first planet from the sun and is currently the smallest planet within our solar system.

• It is tidally locked to the sun, so only one face is facing the sun at all times.

• It orbits the sun every 88 Earth days, but it rotates on its own axis every 176 Earth days.
