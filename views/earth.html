Welcome to Earth!

• Earth is the third planet from the Sun. It has a moon that is tidally locked (one face is only visible from Earth), and it causes the oceanic tides within Earth.

• Interesting fact: The Earth's rotation is slowing down and in about 140 million years the day will increase from 24 hours to 25 hours.

• Currently Earth is the only known planet to support life.
